Introduction

zylon.tk is a forward-thinking network where everyone has the ability to explore and create new things.

Connection

zylon.tk's servers are the basis to the community. We don't restrict players; anyone can join.

  • Network Hub: play.zylon.tk
  • Survival Server: surv-zylon.tk
  • Creative Server: crea-zylon.tk

Community

zylon.tk offers a forum for discussion with other members, powered by Flarum. Our community can be visited here.

Titles

zylon.tk offers ranks on the forum and guild with no extra permissions granted.

Suspended

Users that have been permanently restricted from our servers or community, or have been restricted from staff status are provided with this title.

Emeritus

Staff members that have been selected because of ability are given an honorary title. If they go inactive or resign (i.e. Forum Moderator), they are first-in-line to reinstate.

Branding

zylon.tk is represented with our blue bolt logo. You can download it here.

The bolt should not be re-colored nor it's shape to be manipulated. It should only be used with zylon.tk's official HEX value of #2B2D51, surv-zylon.tk's official HEX value of #E74C3C and crea-zylon.tk's official HEX value of #3498DB.

Footnote 1: The creative and survival server can be accessed from the network's hub at play.zylon.tk, however, the creative and survival domains can be used for instant access.

Footnote 2: Before joining our servers or our community, read the regulations.
