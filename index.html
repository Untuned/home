Getting started

zylon.tk is a forward-thinking network where everyone has the ability to explore and create new things.

Connection

zylon.tk's servers are the basis to the community. We don't restrict players; anyone can join.

  • Network Hub: play.zylon.tk
  • Survival Server: surv-zylon.tk
  • Creative Server: crea-zylon.tk

Note: The creative and survival server can be accessed from the network's hub at play.zylon.tk, however, the creative and survival domains can be used for instant access.
